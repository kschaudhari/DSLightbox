SCENERY

Mount Whitfield, Cairns - Western Australia

There are places where you can clearly hear mother nature speaking to you, providing a huge amount of peace and inspiration. Spectacular rock formation dated millions of years ago, a sensational Quarry and incredible sandy beaches to name a few: explore a world full of beauty and grace and of course, don’t forget your binoculars to feel closer to this great marvel.

THE PINNACLES, AUSTRALIA

If you choose to go to Australia a must stop is Cervantes, to check out the Pinnacles, a crop of limestone needles spread across the Outback like an otherworldly phenomenon.

Out of the yellow desert sands of Nambung National Park, the Pinnacles represent one of Australia's most unique and breath-taking landscapes.

Pinnacle Desert, Perth - Western Australia

LAKE PUKAKI, NEW ZELAND

Don’t be surprised if Lake Pukaki looks familiar – it’s a bona fide movie star, thanks to Peter Jackson, who selected it as his setting for Laketown in the Hobbit trilogy.

Pukaki’s cinematic attributes include: golden tussocks, white-cloaked mountains and pristine waters that colour-shift from vivid turquoise to milky blue. Lording it over the entire scene is New Zealand’s highest peak, Aoraki Mt Cook, which draws mountaineers, hikers and cyclists to its handsome heights (3,724m).

Lake Pukaki, South Island, New Zeland

SINGAPORE QUARRY, SINGAPORE

At the south-western end of the Dairy Farm Nature Park is the famous Singapore Quarry, a remarkable oasis of calm which has been transformed into a wetland habitat.

The location offers sensational views which are worth the trip alone and there’s a viewing platform where the wetland’s cliffs overlook a peaceful lake. Are you a nature lover? Then you will be happy to know that the transformation of the site has attracted many fauna, including the dabchick (also known as the little grebe) as well as numerous species of dragonflies. In addition to them, if looking in the right place, you could spot herons, monkeys, bats and egrets.

Dairy Farm Nature Park, Singapore

AMMOULIANI ISLAND, GREECE

Gawp at the amazing Ammouliani Island on the Mount Athos Gulf of Halkidiki. This is green and verdant, home to flourishing olive plantations, incredible sandy beaches and a turquoies blue sea. Explore a history that goes back centuries on your own, or book up for one of the many excursions to ensure you wander through breath-taking views.

Halkidiki, Kavourotrypes Beach, Greece
